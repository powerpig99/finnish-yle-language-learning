# Privacy Policy - YLE Finnish Learner

YLE Finnish Learner does not collect, store, or sell personal data.

What the extension does:
- It runs locally in your browser to display dual subtitles and learning tools on YLE Areena.
- When you request a translation, the selected text (subtitles or clicked words) is sent directly from your browser to the translation provider you choose (for example Google Translate, DeepL, Claude, Gemini, Grok, or Kimi).
- The developer does not receive this data. There is no developer server.

What is stored locally:
- Your settings (language, toggles, API keys) are stored locally in Chrome sync storage.
- Translation caches are stored locally in your browser to speed up repeat viewing.

What is not collected:
- No tracking or analytics
- No ads
- No sale or sharing of data with third parties

Contact:
If you have questions or requests related to privacy, contact the developer via the Chrome Web Store listing.
